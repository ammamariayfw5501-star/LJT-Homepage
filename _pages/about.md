I am a first-year PhD candidate at [HKUST NLP Group](https://hkustnlp.github.io/), advised by [Professor Junxian He](https://www.cse.ust.hk/~junxianh/). I graduated from [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/) in June 2024.

My research focuses on natural language processing and machine learning. My research interests include:
- LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning
- Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)
- LLM truthfulness and Interpretability
